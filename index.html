About Us

Locally owned and Costa Rican operated, our bakery is a family-run spot where fresh pastries, warm service, and tradition come together. From the moment you walk in, we want you to feel like part of the family.

We specialize in Costa Rican breads and desserts, made fresh daily and packed with authentic flavor. Whether you’re grabbing dessert for a special occasion or enjoying coffee and a pastry in our cozy café, we’re here to bring a little sweetness to your day—beautifully boxed, and always served with care.
